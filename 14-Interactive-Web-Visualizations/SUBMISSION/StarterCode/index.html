Homework Write Up

Our belly buttons play a very important role in the beginning of life. It's the primary entry point for nutrients from a mother to her unborn child. Since the belly button serves such an important role, it's still a part of the body many people don't discuss or pay attention to. This dataset studied several individuals' belly button microbes and the factors that could have influence its growth.

For more information regarding this very insightful dataset, please visit: http://robdunnlab.com/projects/belly-button-biodiversity/
